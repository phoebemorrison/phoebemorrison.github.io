Color Alphabet
This book is the result of months worth of research and investigation into the world of color. For each letter I selected a topic that related to color. The topics range from the work of Alice Neel to Razzle Dazzle Camouflage. I paired my research with digital collages. The final book was printed on coated paper and the text pages were printed on ink jet film.
