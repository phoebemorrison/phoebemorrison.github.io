Postcard Books
I created four packaged envelopes of postcards using scans of film photographs I took while traveling in Europe. The series is a homage to the books of postcards my great great grandmother collected while she was traveling at my age. The photographs were taken in Les Tuileries, Fondation Louis Vuitton, Versailles, and Amsterdam.
